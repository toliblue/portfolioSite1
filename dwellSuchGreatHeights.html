Dwell: Such Great Heights

A selection of works featured in Dwell Magazine, created under Jennifer Siegal of the Office of Mobile Design, in conjuction with Gabriela Schwiezer. While doing more traditional architectural intern work, Gabriela and I had the opportunity to add custom interior design elements to Siegals' newly built modular addition to a 1920's home. The designs include a custom railing, deck design, and a modular playhouse for her daughter.
